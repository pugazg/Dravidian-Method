## About This Work

<div class="about-panel">
	<p>The Dravidian Method is an interpretive essay. It is not an official history, manifesto, or catalogue of achievements.</p>
	<p>It follows one question across nearly a century of political and institutional change: when power over human destiny becomes concentrated, what forms of public action can widen participation?</p>
