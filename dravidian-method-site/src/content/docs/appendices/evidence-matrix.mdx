This book advances an interpretive argument rather than a partisan one. Each chapter combines historical interpretation with publicly verifiable evidence.

## Methodological Note

The book does not claim that every programme succeeded or that every policy achieved its stated objective. It treats evidence as a way to illuminate a larger historical question: how the form of concentration changes across generations.
