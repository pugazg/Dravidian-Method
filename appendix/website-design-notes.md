Appendix D

Website Design Notes

Philosophy

This website should resemble a long-form digital book rather than a political campaign website.

The experience should feel closer to:

* Stripe
* Apple
* Linear
* Vercel
* Medium

than a government portal.

⸻

Design Principles

* Minimal
* Typography first
* Generous whitespace
* Slow reading experience
* Elegant transitions
* No visual clutter

⸻

Typography

Primary font:

* Inter

Reading font:

* Source Serif
* Literata
* IBM Plex Serif

⸻

Colour Palette

Near Black (#111111)

Accent:

Deep Maroon

Secondary Accent:

Muted Gold

Avoid party colours.

The emphasis is intellectual rather than political.
