Each chapter combines historical interpretation with publicly verifiable evidence.

Methodological Note

This book distinguishes between:

* Historical evidence
* Policy evidence
* Interpretive argument

Where evidence remains incomplete, the text explicitly identifies uncertainty rather than presenting speculation as established fact.
